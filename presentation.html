Certificate Automation

Why?

  • No more annual wildcard replacement
  • Save time
  • Improve security
  • Minimize risk of service interruption due to certificate expiration
  • Prepare for quick response to new vulnerabilities in existing keys and encryption/hashing algorithms
- Why do we want to automate certificates?
- Certificates with a shorter lifespan are more secure
- Better key management, by avoiding constant import and export of wildcard laying all over
- With one cert everywhere, one compromise means everything is compromised
